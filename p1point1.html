Topic 1: Peruvian Cuisine

Peruvian Cuisine being one of the most diverse cuisine in the world
that blends indigenous traditions with influence Spanish, African, Chinese,
and Japanese immegrants. Some of their dishes that highlights their cultural
fusion includes ceviche, raw fish cured in lime juice, and lomo saltado, a
stir-fried beef with Chinese flavors, which gained a lot of popularity globally
as a culinary capital.

Topic 2: Chinese Language

The Chinese language, primarily Manderin, is one of the most spoken language
in the world. The language consist of various pitch for each words that can
change the meaning. The language has a history spanning thousands of years
that evolved from oracle bone script to modern simplified and traditional forms.

Topic 3: The Creation of Esports

Esports, that stands for electronic sports, emerged from competitive video
gaming that started with a small scale arcade and LAN tournaments in 1970s-90s.
During the late 1990s and early 2000s, there was a rise of games like StarCraft,
Counter Strike, and Warcraft 3 that built international competitive scenes.
Currently, esports has become a multibillion dollar industry that runs professional
gamers with large sponsorships that rivals tranditional sports in scale and
cultural impact.
